FAQ

  • Home
  • FAQs
What types of solar systems do you install?
We install residential, commercial, and custom-designed solar systems tailored to your specific energy needs.
How long does a solar panel installation take?
Most residential installations take 1–3 days, while commercial systems may take longer depending on size and complexity.
What maintenance is required for solar panels?
Routine inspections, occasional cleaning, and monitoring help maintain peak efficiency, all of which we can handle.
Do you offer solar panel upgrades for existing systems?
Yes, we provide panel upgrades to increase energy output and improve overall system performance.
How does solar battery storage work?
It stores excess solar energy during the day, so you can use it at night or during power outages.
Can I monitor my solar system remotely?
Absolutely. Our system monitoring solutions allow you to track energy generation and usage in real time.
Are your solar systems eligible for government rebates or incentives?
Yes, we can help you identify and apply for local or national solar incentives and rebates.
What’s included in a custom system design?
Custom designs include an energy usage assessment, site analysis, and tailored system layout to match your goals.
What is the lifespan of a solar panel system?
Our systems typically last 25–30 years with proper care and periodic maintenance.
Is solar energy really worth the investment?
Yes, solar significantly reduces energy bills, increases property value, and offers long-term environmental benefits.
Do you offer warranties on your products and services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties on solar panels, inverters, and installation workmanship.
How can I get started with Installed By Us?
Just contact us through our website or call us directly. We'll schedule a consultation and guide you through the next steps.
